Admissions

We welcome applications from talented individuals who share our passion for learning and growth. Explore our admission requirements and application process to join our vibrant community.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirement of 3.0
  • Standardized test scores (SAT or ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application
  2. Submit all required documents
  3. Pay the application fee
  4. Wait for the admission decision
  5. Accept the offer and complete enrollment
